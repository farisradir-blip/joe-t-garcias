A Family Legacy Since 1935

Joe T. Garcia's Mexican Restaurant was established on July 4, 1935 by Mr. and Mrs. Joe T. Garcia in a small home on Commerce Street. What began as a modest 16-seat eatery has grown into one of Fort Worth's most beloved institutions.

By the 1970s, Joe T. Garcia's had become one of the most popular restaurants in the Dallas-Fort Worth area. Today, the Lancarte Family continues to share their special family history with every guest who walks through the door.

Known for our mouthwatering old-school recipes, large dining areas, and famous garden patio, customers happily wait in long lines just to experience the magic of Joe T. Garcia's.
